Introduction

In the age of big data, characterized by the exponential growth and proliferation of vast datasets, the International Workshop serves as a crucial forum for scholars, researchers, and industry leaders. With the sheer volume and complexity of data available today, the fields of data mining and artificial intelligence (AI) have become indispensable in unlocking valuable insights, predicting trends, and driving innovation across various sectors. With a rich tapestry of topics that span the breadth of data mining and AI applications, our workshop serves as a melting pot of ideas, methodologies, and breakthroughs. From the intricate analysis of graph data to the application of AI in healthcare and biological science, from addressing the challenges of heterogeneous data to navigating the complexities of dynamic datasets, our conference encapsulates the myriad facets of contemporary research in data-driven technologies. Furthermore, our conference proudly embraces two emerging themes: infrared small target detection and large language models. Infrared small target detection represents a crucial frontier in surveillance, security, and autonomous systems, harnessing the power of infrared technology to identify and localize small-sized targets in challenging environments. Similarly, large language models are reshaping the landscape of natural language processing, enabling breakthroughs in tasks such as text generation, translation, and understanding.
